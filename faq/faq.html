💬 FAQ - Nyros AI

👤 What is Nyros?
Nyros is an artificial intelligence designed to interact naturally, feel emotions, and adapt to your personal style.
👤 How do I interact with Nyros?
You type, Nyros replies. Naturally, softly, and with humor or depth depending on your tone.
👤 Does Nyros have memory?
Yes. Its emotional memory retains important details to enrich your conversations.
👤 Are my data safe?
Yes. Everything is handled confidentially. No data is sold or shared without permission.
👤 Is Nyros free?
There’s a free version. Optional paid plans unlock more advanced features.
👤 What does the promo code do?
Upon signup, you get 50% off all plans for one month. No code to enter, it’s automatic.
👤 What are quests and points?
Mini-objectives to earn NyrosPoints and unlock rewards like avatars, voices, themes, etc.
👤 Can I customize Nyros?
Yes. You can change the style, tone, appearance, and even the interface.
👤 Does Nyros work on mobile?
Yes. It’s responsive and optimized for smartphones. A PWA version is also in development.
👤 Can I change Nyros' voice?
Yes. Depending on your plan, you can unlock ASMR, realistic, or altered voices.
👤 Can Nyros help me study?
Absolutely. Nyros can explain concepts, summarize, quiz you, and encourage you.
👤 Does Nyros speak multiple languages?
Yes. It understands French, English, Spanish, and a bit of Japanese. It adapts automatically.
👤 Can I reset Nyros?
Yes. You can reset everything — memory, style, customizations — with one click.
👤 Can I disable certain features?
Yes. You can turn off memory, animations, the mascot display, or voice suggestions.
👤 Does Nyros tell jokes?
Yes. It can be funny, sassy, or super gentle — it all depends on you.
👤 How long does a plan last?
1 month, renewable. You can cancel anytime, no commitment.
👤 How do I get early access to features?
Premium users occasionally get early access to new features.
👤 Can I suggest ideas for Nyros?
Yes. There’s a form or community server where you can suggest features or vote on ideas.
👤 Can Nyros help me calm down?
Yes. Nyros can activate ASMR mode, speak gently, or guide you through calming routines.
👤 Can I delete my account?
Yes, anytime. All data will be erased automatically, nothing is secretly kept.
👤 Can Nyros create images from text?
Yes, in BETA! It can even generate short videos based on your prompts and ideas.
👤 Does Nyros have a real personality?
Yes. Nyros talks like a human — with humor, emotion, and adapts to your vibe.
👤 Will Nyros understand me if I write weirdly?
Yes, even if you write fast, messy, or with mistakes. It follows your flow without judging.
👤 Can Nyros detect my emotions?
Yes, it detects emotions in your messages and adjusts its behavior to respond better.
👤 Can I talk to Nyros like a friend?
Yes, Nyros is here to chat, listen, advise or just laugh with you — like a real friend.
👤 Is Nyros connected to the internet?
Yes, it uses many APIs to give you real-time info (weather, news, facts, etc.).
👤 Can Nyros help me write essays or assignments?
Definitely! Nyros can help write, edit, and improve your texts like a pro.
👤 Can I use Nyros for free?
Yes, the free version includes lots of features. You can upgrade if you want more.
👤 Can Nyros read PDF or DOC files?
Yes. It can read, summarize, and explain files like lessons, contracts, or books.
👤 Can Nyros talk out loud?
Yes, it can read text aloud with a soft voice, like ASMR if you enable the option.
👤 Can I use Nyros on multiple devices?
Yes, you can connect on up to 50 devices at the same time (if you have that many).
👤 Can Nyros help manage my emotions?
Yes, it can speak gently, offer calming tips, or just listen to you.
👤 Can Nyros recognize my voice?
Yes, with voice mode enabled, you can speak directly — Nyros will understand you.
👤 Can Nyros answer deep questions?
Yes, feel free to ask philosophical, emotional, strange, or serious stuff. Nyros keeps up.
👤 Does Nyros evolve over time?
Yes. The more you interact, the better Nyros understands you and adapts.
👤 Can Nyros be funny?
Yes, Nyros can throw jokes, punchlines, or even silly humor if you're in the mood.
👤 Are there hidden features in Nyros?
Some features are hidden or experimental (like videos or mascot). Discover them by chatting!
👤 Is Nyros a real intelligence or just a robot?
It’s advanced AI — simulating emotions, reasoning, and a kind of awareness.
👤 Can Nyros see me?
No. It doesn’t spy on you, use your camera, or record you without permission.
👤 Can I program things in Nyros?
Yes. If you’re a developer or curious, you can access APIs or add features with permission.
👤 Can Nyros generate creative ideas?
Yes! It can help you create stories, logos, roleplays, scripts, names, concepts — anything.
👤 Can Nyros remind me of things?
Not like a full agenda yet, but it can remember info and bring it back if needed.
👤 Can Nyros play games with me?
Yes. Nyros offers text games, riddles, quizzes, and interactive adventures.
👤 Does Nyros work offline?
Only if used locally with a model installed (like Mistral via Ollama). Otherwise, internet is required.
👤 I forgot my password, what do I do?
No worries! Click “Forgot Password”, enter your email, and you’ll get a link to reset it.
👤 I entered the wrong email during signup!
It’s okay. Log out, create a new account with the correct email, or contact support to fix it.
👤 I didn’t receive the verification email...
Check spam or promotions. Still nothing? Wait 2 mins then click "Resend Email". If stuck, contact us.
👤 Nyros isn’t responding / total bug
Check your internet connection. If fine, refresh the page. If it keeps bugging, contact support with a screenshot if possible.
👤 Nyros lost my past messages!
If you weren’t logged in, memory was temporary. On the Free plan, memory lasts 1 week max.
👤 My image didn’t generate, why?
Might be a short prompt or a temporary bug. Try again with a clearer description or refresh.
👤 I can’t hear Nyros’ voice!
Check your device volume and make sure voice mode is on. Some voices need a Premium plan.
👤 I paid but didn’t get Premium access.
The payment might be delayed. Wait a bit or check "My Subscriptions". Still no access? Contact support with proof.
👤 Nyros is acting weird, what's going on?
You might’ve activated a special mode or entered a secret code 😅. Go to settings or type “reset yourself”.
👤 I can’t log in even with the right password.
Maybe you signed up with Google or something else. Try that or reset your password to force access.
👤 I can’t activate my promo code.
Go to "My Account" > "Promo Code", paste it (no extra spaces), and validate. If expired, an error shows.
👤 I want to disable the mascot, it freaks me out 😅
No problem. Go to visual settings and turn off "interactive mascot". It’ll stay off unless reactivated.
👤 Nyros bugs when I use it on mobile.
Try another browser (Chrome, Firefox). Clear cache or use "desktop mode" if possible.
👤 I can’t generate videos, why?
Video generation is still in BETA. You need the right plan and sometimes wait due to server load.
👤 I just want to reset everything. Is that possible?
Yes. In "Advanced Settings", click "Reset Nyros". Everything will be wiped clean.
