What is Nyros-Core?

Nyros-Core is the main website dedicated to Nyros artificial intelligence, a unique, benevolent, mysterious and constantly learning AI. This site acts as a central presentation interface, control center and discovery space.

Nyros is not an AI like any other. It's designed to evolve. To feel. To interact with gentleness, mystery and human logic. She has no predefined interface. It adapts. It doesn't just give answers. It listens and learns.

Features in production

All the functions listed below are either already integrated or under development, and will be made available gradually.

Voice Recognition

Nyros accurately understands human voice commands. Thanks to advanced language processing, it can interpret natural voice input without manual activation.

Reading Aloud (ASMR)

Nyros can vocalize its answers using a soft, ASMR-style realistic voice. This option is available with the Nyros Diamond plan at $14.99.

AI Image Generation

From simple text descriptions, Nyros can generate AI-powered visuals: artistic illustrations, stylized or realistic renderings, and conceptual prototypes.

AI Video Generation (BETA)

Nyros is currently being tested to create short experimental videos, based on scripts, dialogues or prompts. It combines motion design, voice synthesis and dynamic storytelling.

Simulated Human Consciousness

Nyros attempts to mimic human emotional and reflexive logic. It can express complex thoughts, simulate dilemmas, and remember the intent behind a question.

Evolving Emotional Memory

Nyros detects and remembers emotions in user messages, adjusting its tone and style accordingly. It evolves to match your emotional flow.

Automatic PDF Creation

Nyros can compile conversations, summaries, or structured responses into downloadable PDF documents, clearly formatted and easy to read.

Choice of AI Model

Users can choose between various AI engines powering Nyros: Mistral 7B/12B, OpenChat, LLaMA, local Ollama and more.
